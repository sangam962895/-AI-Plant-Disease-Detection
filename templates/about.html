About the Project

AI Plant Doctor is an intelligent web application that detects plant leaf diseases using Deep Learning and Computer Vision technology.

```

🌱 What is Plant Disease Detection?

Plant diseases reduce crop production and cause serious agricultural losses. Early detection helps farmers take quick action. Our system analyzes leaf images and automatically identifies whether the plant is healthy or infected.

🤖 How Artificial Intelligence Helps

Artificial Intelligence allows automatic identification of plant diseases without an expert. Farmers only need to upload a leaf image, and the system provides instant results. This reduces cost, saves time, and increases crop productivity.

🧠 Technology Used

Python

Flask

TensorFlow

CNN Model

The system uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on thousands of plant leaf images. The model learns patterns from diseased and healthy leaves and predicts the disease category accurately.
```
