How The AI Model Works

Simple enough for anyone to understand, powerful enough to rival expert botanists

Image Upload

You upload a clear photo of the plant leaf affected by disease. Our system accepts PNG, JPG, GIF, and WebP formats up to 10MB.

Image Processing

The image is processed and normalized to a standard size (224x224 pixels) for consistent analysis by our neural network.

AI Prediction

Our CNN model analyzes patterns in the leaf and identifies the disease. The model was trained on 40+ different plant diseases.

Results & Treatment

You receive the disease name, confidence percentage, and detailed treatment steps to save your plant.

What is a CNN?

A Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) is an AI model inspired by how our brains process images. It learns by looking at thousands of examples.

The Learning Process:
  1. 1. Model sees healthy leaf → learns "healthy" features
  2. 2. Model sees diseased leaf → learns disease patterns
  3. 3. Repeats with millions of examples
  4. 4. Now it can identify unseen leaves accurately

Think of it like teaching a student: show enough examples, and they learn to identify patterns on their own.
