Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How accurate is the detection?

Our AI model achieves 95%+ accuracy in detecting plant diseases. For best results, use clear, well-lit images of the affected leaf.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, and WebP formats. Maximum file size is 10MB. Images should be at least 224x224 pixels.

How many plant diseases can you detect?

We can detect over 40 common plant diseases affecting crops and ornamental plants globally. Coverage varies by region.

Is my data stored or shared?

No. We do not store images or share your data with third parties. All analysis is done locally and securely.
